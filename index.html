About the lab

Brief description

MT SpinLab is the computational spintronics and magnetism research group of Prof. Md. Torikul Islam in the Department of Physics, Khulna University.

Current work spans spin‑orbit‑torque‑driven switching, domain‑wall dynamics in altermagnets, and the thermal stability of cylindrical magnetic tunnel junctions for low‑power memory applications.
